Our Story

Food District is an urban upgraded fast casual restaurant forged in the heart of Beirut indulging its clients with a unique culinary offering from around the world. Food District's lust for innovation has created several sub brands specialized in cultural cuisines. Food Districts successful sub brands include sould food, Taco Diablos, Aloha Poke, City Wok, Fiori, and Sweet Greens.
